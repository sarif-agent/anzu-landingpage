More About Anzu

AnzuJaamu, also known simply as Anzu, is a Turkish cosplayer from Ankara, Turkey. The nickname Anzu is a Japanese word that means "Apricot." Anzu is known for her unique fashion style, including "Creepy-cute, fairy-kei, Japanese street fashion," and has been actively cosplaying since 2014. Anzu runs a fashion and cosplay blog dedicated to showcasing her costumes and outfits, as well as featuring items from her sponsors. She is also recognized for her exceptional makeup skills. Additionally, Anzu frequently participates in cosplay events, either as a jury member or an honored guest. Despite being one of the best cosplayers in Turkey, Anzu has gained international recognition for her talent in the world of cosplay.
